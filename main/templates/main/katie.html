Katie Bloudoff-Indelicato

When her parents, Robert Indelicato and Leslie Bloudoff, produced the first bottle of Serendipity Sparkling Wine, Katie was thrilled. Thrilled because this was indeed the best sparkling wine that she had tasted, and because it came with the announcement that California Wine Classics was opening its own winery to feature their new brand. For as long as she could remember, Robert and Leslie had been managing their vineyard, and making and selling wine overseas. But this sparkling wine program meant a new direction: bricks, mortar and serendipitous opportunities. Katie recently graduated with her MBA and will now join the family business. Having studied wine distribution and marketing in graduate school, California Wine Classics is putting her to work marketing and branding their new sparkling wine product. While spare time is a rare occurrence, Katie love exploring new restaurants, national parks and taking spontaneous road trips. With her new position, she is eager to develop and expand the sparkling wine division and share this outstanding wine with her customers.
Contact Katie at katie@californiawineclassics.com.
