Serendipity Tasting Notes

Serendipity, a Blanc de Blanc 2014 vintage sparkling wine is produced in the Méthode Champenoise style, utilizing Chardonnay grapes from Sierra Vista Vineyards picked at 18 brix. This wine is dry with a hint of sweetness. The creamy texture continues to develop as the wine ages en tirage. A toasty bouquet of yeast from aging over 2 - 2-1/2 years in the bottle means that the suppleness mid-palate finishes with a bright, crisp impression. Tiny bubbles signify the exceptional quality of this product and never fail to deliver in both flavor and aroma.
