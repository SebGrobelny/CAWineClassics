Robert Indelicato

Throughout his childhood, Robert spent summers and his hours after school helping his grandfather in the vineyard, his uncle with the tank and barrel room, and his father with the forklift. After earning his enology degree, Robert produced and sold wine through his family's winery, but true to his entrepreneurial spirit, he sought to operate his own business--California Wine Classics--initiated in 2006.
Today, Robert applies his extensive experience in viticulture, winemaking and both national and international distribution with every client. Having worked with and served his customers for over 30 years, his true skill lies in his ability to match each client's need with a great wine and fulfill orders within often tight deadlines. Be it a small lot order, a customized blend with a quick turnaround, or a source of sought after California wine for year-after-year programs, Robert works with each customer to develop solutions to fit their needs.
Robert also enjoys watching his beloved SF Giants, sharing life advice with his kids, checking on the grapevines, enjoying a glass of wine with a good book. After so many years, wine is truly a part of his blood! Ever the winemaker, Robert often sets up rows of glasses for family taste tests. Having produced an exquisite sparkling wine, Robert continually searches for improvement on both his still and sparking wine products.
Contact Robert at robert@californiawineclassics.com or +1 (209) 727-0396.
