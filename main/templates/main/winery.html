Winery

California Wine Classics works with local growers, wineries and distributors to bring you some of California’s extraordinary wine. To date, we’ve filled hundreds of shipping containers, bottled thousands of case goods and delivered the perfect product to meet the needs of your exclusive program. In our effort to continue to serve you, California Wine Classics opened our first winery tasting room in Lodi’s renovated Old Ice House.

Our location at 27 Locust Street in Lodi is more than four walls. It’s the result of years of planning and success! Our new facility allows us to meet the needs of our customers effectively and efficiently, and--meet you face-to-face.

Rapid response

Customers come to us with a range of timelines in order to meet their own deadlines. While some may need one order per year, others require regular shipments over an extended period of time. Our facility provides us with the opportunity to expand both our bulk wine product line as well as our shiner case goods. This allows us to minimize the amount of time it takes to make just the right selection to meet your program’s needs.

Introducing Serendipity Sparkling Wine

In 2014, California Wine Classics produced its first vintage of Serendipity Sparkling Wine, a Méthode Champenoise Blanc de Blanc. This first lot sourced Chardonnay grapes from our own Sierra Vista Vineyards in the rolling hills east of Lodi. After crushing, the juice was fermented and produced at a specialized facility in Lake County. Beginning with the 2017 harvest, California Wine Classics crushed the Chardonnay locally and are now fermenting and producing this vintage of Serendipity Sparkling Wine here in our Lodi facility. If you’re looking to add a sparkling wine product to your program, contact us or click here to learn more about our Sparkling Wine products.
